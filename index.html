Sustainable Agriculture

Our work includes developing novel materials that can enhance nutrient delivery and reduce environmental impact, contributing to a more resilient food supply chain.

Water Purification Technologies

We focus on designing non-noble metal catalysts that can effectively remove contaminants from water, making purification processes more accessible and affordable. This directly aligns with our publication on photocatalytic H2O2 production for water treatment.

Renewable Energy & Storage

This pillar covers our efforts in CO2 conversion to fuels and the development of high-capacity anodes for Li-ion batteries. Our goal is to create multifunctional materials that can both generate and store clean energy efficiently.
